about us

We are a locally owned and home-grown full-service cabinet manufacturer in Lake County, FL. We serve the tri-county area of Marion, Sumter, and Lake counties, and we have been providing quality products and services for over 35 years.

FROM START TO FINISH

WE TAKE PRIDE IN PROVIDING CUSTOMERS WITH QUALITY SERVICE FROM THE BEGINNING DESIGNS, TO THE MANAGEMENT OF THE PROJECT, THEN TO INSTALLATION, AND BEYOND!

Portfolio

We at Home-Art strive to demonstrate both hard work and professionalism. Our faith in God has guided the company through the years, providing it with steady business and committed personnel. This has led to the completion of thousands of projects and friendships. 

Until the formation of this website our business has been mostly “word of mouth” recommendations from former satisfied customers. We think this speaks volumes of our product and installation quality. We look forward to transforming your home improvement dreams into reality.

our story

  • 1982 - Fruitland Park, FL

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Established in 1981 by father, son, and son-in-law. Individually and mutually, we envisioned the opportunity in the home improvement industry, and we combined years of knowledge and expertise to set the bar for quality cabinetry in the Central Florida area. For 40 years, we have been providing the highest quality products that innovation and craftsmanship can provide.

  • ...

    Building a Brand

    We didn't become an overnight sensation, but we're not a one hit wonder either. Through hard work and dedication, we've earned the trust of our neighbors and of local leaders in the design and building industries.

  • ...

    A Market Leader

    We take pride in our position as a market leader, not because we have a special amount of influence or power, but because we have developed meaningful relationships with the customers and community we serve.
